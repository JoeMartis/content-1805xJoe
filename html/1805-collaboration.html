Collaboration

MIT has a culture of teamwork. We encourage you to work with study partners.
  • Collaboration on homework is encouraged.
  • You must write your solutions yourself, in your own words.
  • You must list all collaborators and outside sources of information.

Clicker Abuse

We do not anticipate having this come up, but, sadly, we have been made aware that there are cases where some students use a clicker for an absent student. It should go without saying that this is a major violation of academic integrity. If we become aware of it happening there will be serious consequences, including the loss of all clicker points for both parties.

Please see MIT Academic Integrity: Consequences
